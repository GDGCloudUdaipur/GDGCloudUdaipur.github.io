About GDG Cloud Udaipur

Google Developer Group (GDG) Cloud Udaipur is a dynamic and inclusive community for developers, students, and tech enthusiasts passionate about Google's developer technologies and cloud platforms. Our mission is to foster a local ecosystem where individuals can learn, share knowledge, and connect with like-minded peers.

We focus on exploring the vast potential of Google Cloud Platform (GCP), innovative AI/ML technologies like Gemini, serverless solutions with Firebase, Android development, modern Web technologies, and other cutting-edge tools from Google. Through engaging tech talks, hands-on study jams, practical workshops, and networking events, we aim to empower our members with the skills and insights needed to excel in the ever-evolving tech landscape.

Join us to be part of a vibrant local tech community, network with peers and industry experts, contribute your expertise, and stay at the forefront of technological advancements. Everyone is welcome to learn and grow with us!
