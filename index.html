About GDG Cloud Udaipur

Google Developer Group (GDG) Cloud Udaipur is a community of developers, students, and tech enthusiasts interested in Google's developer technologies and cloud platforms. We aim to learn, share, and connect through various events like tech talks, study jams, hands-on workshops, and more.

Our focus is on exploring the vast potential of Google Cloud Platform (GCP), AI/ML technologies like Gemini, Firebase, Android, Web technologies, and other cutting-edge innovations from Google. We believe in fostering an inclusive environment where everyone is welcome to contribute and grow their skills.

Join us to be a part of a dynamic local tech community, network with peers and industry experts, and stay updated with the latest in the world of technology.
