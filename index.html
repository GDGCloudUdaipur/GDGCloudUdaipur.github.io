About Us

GDG Cloud Udaipur is a community of developers, students, and tech enthusiasts passionate about Google Cloud Platform and open-source technologies.

Our mission is to create a vibrant, inclusive learning environment where members can share knowledge, collaborate on projects, and grow their skills. We host regular meetups, workshops, and study jams covering a wide range of topics from cloud infrastructure and serverless computing to AI, machine learning, and big data.

Join us to connect with fellow tech lovers, learn from industry experts, and be a part of the future of technology in Udaipur.
