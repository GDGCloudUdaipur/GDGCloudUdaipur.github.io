4. Process for Requesting a Refund

To request a refund based on the conditions outlined above, please send an email to abhinav.jain1411@gmail.com. Your email must include:

Refunds, where applicable, will be processed to the original method of payment (wherever feasible) within 7-14 business days after the refund request has been approved by the GDG Cloud Udaipur organizers.
